# OFFERS AND PRICING

## Core principle
Pricing should map to cash impact, not technical complexity. Buyers do not care how modular the codebase is. Tragic, but consistent.

## Offer 1: Revenue Leak Audit
**Format:** one-off
**Price:** 99 to 249 EUR
**For:** founders, creators, local service operators
**Includes:**
- intake review
- lead or offer data analysis
- prioritized report
- next-step recommendations

## Offer 2: Lead Recovery Setup
**Format:** setup service
**Price:** 300 to 900 EUR
**Includes:**
- workflow configuration
- scoring rules
- report templates
- recovery queue generation
- one revision cycle

## Offer 3: Weekly Recovery Engine
**Format:** recurring subscription
**Price:** 79 to 299 EUR per month
**Includes:**
- recurring workflow run
- weekly report
- ranked opportunity queue
- improvement notes

## Offer 4: Custom Workflow Build
**Format:** premium custom implementation
**Price:** from 1500 EUR
**Includes:**
- niche-specific workflow design
- custom configs
- custom reporting
- operator documentation

### Solo consultant or coach
- audit: 149 EUR
- setup: 500 EUR

### Agency or high-ticket operator
- audit: 249 EUR
- setup: 900 EUR
- monthly: 299 EUR

## Simple qualification rules
Sell the offer only if:
- the client already has leads or audience data
- missed follow-up is a real problem
- there is clear money attached to faster or smarter action

Do not prioritize prospects who:
- have no traffic, no leads, and no active offer
- want “AI magic” with no business process
- refuse to define the value of a recovered lead

## Packaging line
Use language like:
- recover missed opportunities
- rank the leads most worth chasing
- generate a weekly action queue
- reduce follow-up chaos

Avoid language like:
- autonomous intelligence mesh
- multi-agent ecosystem
- orchestration fabric

## Upgrade path
1. sell audit
2. convert audit into setup
3. convert setup into subscription
4. upsell custom workflow stack

## Minimum target economics
A realistic first target could be:
- 4 audits at 149 EUR = 596 EUR
- 2 setups at 500 EUR = 1000 EUR
- 2 monthly clients at 129 EUR = 258 EUR MRR

That is enough to validate the repo as a commercial nucleus before expanding scope.
