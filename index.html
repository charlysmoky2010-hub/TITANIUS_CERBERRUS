TITANIUS_CERBERRUS is now framed as an oceanic interface for a growing octopus intelligence. The long vision is a many-armed operator with memory, market sensing, crypto instinct, and business reflexes. The first public tentacle remains practical: lead_recovery_audit.

Why this repository is narrow on purpose
